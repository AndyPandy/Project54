Everything you need

So many ways to
verify it's you

From push notifications to hardware tokens, Duo supports every method your team needs to stay secure — without the friction.

Multi-Factor Authentication

Add a powerful second layer of security to every login. Push, SMS, phone call, or hardware token — users choose what works best.

Device Trust

Continuously assess device health before granting access. Block outdated, compromised, or unknown devices — automatically.

Single Sign-On

One login for everything. Give employees secure, one-click access to all their apps — cloud or on-prem — from any device.

Remote Access

Secure your workforce wherever they are. Duo's VPN-less access solution ensures only the right people reach the right resources.

Risk-Based Authentication

Intelligently step up authentication based on risk signals. Frictionless for low-risk, harder for suspicious logins.

Ask Duo Anything

AI-powered security assistant that answers questions, diagnoses issues, and guides your team through incidents in plain English.
